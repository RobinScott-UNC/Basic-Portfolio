About Me

I like to think of myself as an upbeat person who sees the positive in everything. My years of customer service and troubleshooting software and interface issues have created a "can do, can accomplish" attitude in all aspects of life. I am enjoying the challenges that Coding Boot Camp brings.


Because these challenges use quite a bit of left brain, I try to exercise my right brain in my spare time. I love gardening, the outdoors, travel, and any type of art or craft object that sparks my imagination and creativity.
